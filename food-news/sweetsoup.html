Hue Specialty Sweet Soup

Both Hue and Hue people have a gentle, quiet but simple beauty. But Hue sweet soup is not like that either. The variety and vibrant colors, each bean, cake or jelly are the quintessence of both royal and rustic cuisine. Coming to Hue is to try Hue sweet soup, not only once but many times, to satisfy the craving box and feel the culture here in the most authentic way.

1. Chè Hẻm

Many people say that when coming to Hue, you must have a cup of Che Hem. Located in a peaceful small alley in the old capital, this sweet soup shop has existed for more than 2 decades and has become the memory of many generations of students and students. Here, you can try the cool bean sweet food, lumpy coconut filter powder or the famous roasted pork filter powder. So famous, but the service here is not appreciated, so you may be a bit surprised when you visit.

Address: At 1 Lane 29, Hung Vuong Street,Hue.
Opening hour: 10h15 – 21h.
Price: 10,000 - 15,000 VND/cup

2. Chè Ngọc Hiền

Along Tran Hung Dao Street, there are a lot of sweet soup shop, but Ngoc Hien sweet soup is still the most crowded place. At first glance, visitors are easily "loved" by sweet soup cabinets with up to 20 eye-catching and extremely stimulating dishes. Sweet soup flavor has been changed to suit the majority of guests, so it no longer retains the characteristics of Hue royal sweet soup. But no matter what kind of sweet soup is full of abundance and deliciousness, along with the price is also extremely cute. The owner is enthusiastic, making clothes is also very fast and professional.

At 65 Tran Hung Dao street.
Opening hour: 14h – 22h.
Price: 10,000 - 15,000 VND/cup

3. Chè Ông Lạc

It is also a perennial sweet soup shop like Che Hem, but on the outside, Ong Lac sweet soup is much simpler and simpler. The shop mainly serves soybean sweet soup with coconut water, sweet and creamy snacks. Holding a simple cup of sweet soup, sitting in the middle of the house in the alley, feeling like returning to the day when he was a child with him and his grandmother in the countryside. Because there is no signboard, if you do not pay close attention, you will find it difficult to find a shop. At this point, do not hesitate to ask people for directions.

At 36 Thanh Tinh street.
Opening hour: 12h30 – 21h.
Price: 5,000 - 11,000 VND/cup

4. Chè gánh

Che ganh is not a specific name of a sweet soup shop, but it is a very gentle and cute feature of Hue culinary culture. Small sweet soup bags, consisting of only a few sweet soup pots, placed on top of two staggered pots, fit neatly under the scaffold. The aunties who sell sweet soup are also cute and the cup of sweet soup is so delicious, cheap and sweet.

Hue Citadel, sidewalks Tran Phu, Nguyen Cong Tru, Dong Ba market,...
Opening hour: 6h - 23h
Price: 5,000 - 15,000 VND/cup

5. Chè Cung Đình

Hue royal sweet soup is made from natural ingredients and aromas such as beans (beans), lotus seeds, young rice, sticky rice, fruits (fruits), sea jelly, and refined sugar. pure, strawberry juice, orange, lemon, chocolate, cocoa ..., many delicious flavors, containing many nutrients to meet the health needs of all ages, bringing body refreshment for all people.

Not everyone can do it to cook delicious Hue Imperial sweet soup pots. All are carefully done right from the raw material preparation. The ingredients used to cook sweet soup must be completely clean, fresh and of good quality. The cooking of sweet soup also has to follow step by step. sweet soup must be experienced in cooking to have its own taste and proportion. Each type of sweet soup also has different requirements. Genuine Hue royal sweet soup has its own secrets to create a dish that is both elegant and luxurious.

Hue Citadel, sidewalks Tran Phu, Nguyen Cong Tru, Dong Ba market,...
Opening hour: 6h - 23h
Price: 5,000 - 15,000 VND/cup
