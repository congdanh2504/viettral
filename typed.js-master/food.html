vietnam - a paradise of food!

Vietnam tourism has significantly gained in popularity over the last few years. And why not? Amazing landscapes, jewel-studded waters, the romance of a pastoral life and impressive architecture – for those struck with wanderlust, Vietnam is paradise.But if you were to consider Vietnam visits worthy merely for its natural beauty, or cultural heritage, you’d be doing the country a disservice. Because, Vietnam tourism is incomplete without trying the food of Vietnam!

Diverse Food Culture

In addition, the cuisine is not only about the delicious dishes, it also expresses the identity of each region and each country. Vietnam is a country located in the tropical monsoon climatic region with the territory divided into 3 regions: North – Center – South and with 54 ethnic groups. It is also perhaps because of characteristics such as geography, climate, culture, customs, etc. That the cuisine of this S-shaped country is also more varied.
